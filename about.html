About This App

This app will inform you about the weather and details of the city/country that you search for. You just have to input the city/country name in the search bar and click on search. On doing so, weather information will appear, with a further "Show More" button. On clicking the "Show More" button, you will be shown information about the country. You can hide it by clicking on the "Hide Details" button. This App has been created by Ayesha Binte Hasan, ID: 2020148.
